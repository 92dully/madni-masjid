About

Madni Masjid serves the local Muslim community in Glasgow. The masjid provides space for daily prayers, Jumu’ah, Qur’an learning, and local community support.

We aim to be a calm and welcoming space for worship and learning, rooted in the everyday needs of those who attend.

The masjid currently provides prayer facilities for men only.
